Did you know?

A single blood donation can help save up to three lives.

Your body has about 5 liters of blood, which makes up 7-8% of your body weight.

The body replaces the donated blood volume (plasma) within 48 hours.

About 1 in 7 people entering a hospital will need a blood transfusion.

O-negative is the universal red cell donor type and is always in high demand.

You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks).

A single car accident victim can require as many as 100 units of blood.

Many premature infants need blood transfusions to survive.

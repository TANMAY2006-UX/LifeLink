About Us

LifeLink is a youth-led initiative founded by the NSS volunteers of Don Bosco Institute of [cite_start]Technology. Our mission is to bridge the gap between blood donors and those in urgent need within [cite_start]our college community and beyond[cite: 1179]. By leveraging technology, we create a network of verified donors, [cite_start]ensuring quick and seamless access to life-saving blood during emergencies[cite: 1180]. We are passionate about [cite_start]promoting a culture of voluntary blood donation and raising awareness about its vital importance[cite: 1181].

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a self-sufficient and highly responsive blood donation ecosystem within [cite_start]our community, a model that can be replicated across India[cite: 1186, 1187]. We envision a future where no one in our network suffers due to a lack of available blood, where technology seamlessly connects donors and recipients, and where the act of giving blood is a celebrated part of our campus [cite_start]culture[cite: 1187]. We aim to achieve this by consistently expanding our donor base, organizing regular [cite_start]donation drives, and fostering a strong sense of social responsibility among students and staff[cite: 1188].

We believe that by starting within our college, we can create a powerful ripple effect that inspires other institutions and organizations to join the cause, ultimately contributing to a [cite_start]blood-secure India[cite: 1188].
